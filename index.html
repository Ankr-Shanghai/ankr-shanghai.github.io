    Overview #

    What is AA Wallet ? #

    AA Wallet is a smart contract wallet solution that supports on-chain email social recovery. Using UniPass Wallet, developers can deliver a seedless, gas-free user experience familiar to Web2 users, significantly increasing conversion rates.

    AA Wallet can help users manage private keys conveniently without using mnemonics and other tools that are unfamiliar and error-prone to Web2 users. AA Wallet aims to help more people become true citizens of Web3 and have a decentralized identity (DID) without compromising on security and censorship resistance.

    With our state-of-the-art on-chain email social recovery solution, users can choose someone with no Web3 experience as a guardian for their account who can help users with social recovery simply using their email account. Due to the on-chain implementation, no third-party servers are required to achieve social recovery.

    AA Wallet aims to provide the most secure and convenient wallet experience using the latest innovations in the field.

    Features of AA Wallet #

    Seedless #

    User uses email to register and login with their accounts. No need to use secret phrase and private key!

    Censorship resistance #

    Users have full control of their accounts without relying on any third-party service.

    Gasless #

    You can pay the gas fee for your users to achieve a truly gasless experience. Users can also pay the gas fee in any token.

    On-chain Email recovery #

    Users can set up guardians using only their emails. When account recovery is needed, they can easily do so by asking their guardians to submit emails appropriately.
